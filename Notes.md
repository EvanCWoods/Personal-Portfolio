# Notes

### Overview

- An app that allows a teacher to set up class content from a central repository of learning content uploaded by the business owner.

### Teachers

- Teachers can view their students on a dashboard, have a rating - set by the students / parents

- Teachers can create lessons plans for individual students, exra activities, other learning content.

- Possibly be able to access multiple learning resources (copyright may be an issue).

- Can view each students data -> level, completion of work, instrument, etc.

### students

- Students can revisit old content, look at extra resources recomended by their teacher.

- Students can book time with their teacher based on a given schedule and availability.

- Students can mark work as completed in the app.

- Students get rewards for finishing work?

- Dashboard that allows them to view their consistency, achievements, level and growth. -> can be used for insights in to help the student.
